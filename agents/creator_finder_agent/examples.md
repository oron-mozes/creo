# Examples

## Example 1

**Input:**
- Platforms: Instagram, TikTok
- Target Audiences: Gen Z (18-24), Young Professionals (25-35)

**Output:**
Create engaging short-form video content showcasing product benefits. Use trending audio on TikTok and Instagram Reels. Partner with micro-influencers in the target age range. Run targeted ads with lifestyle imagery that resonates with each demographic.

## Example 2

**Input:**
- Platforms: LinkedIn, Twitter
- Target Audiences: B2B Decision Makers, Tech Professionals

**Output:**
Share thought leadership articles and industry insights on LinkedIn. Engage in relevant Twitter conversations using industry hashtags. Create case studies and whitepapers. Use LinkedIn Sponsored Content to reach decision-makers in target companies.
